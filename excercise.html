When excercising your dog / puppy, care must be taken to maintain a safe level of impact on the dog. As puppies should avoid high impact excercise while their joints and bones are still developing.

Excercises:

Low Impact:

  • Walking
  • Swimming
  • Gentle Play
  • Obedience Training

Medium Impact:

  • Jogging
  • Hiking
  • Fetch
  • Tug-o-War

High Impact:

  • Sprinting
  • Jumping
  • Rough Play
  • Bite Training
